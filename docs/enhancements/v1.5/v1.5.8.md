## Stage 8.4 — Add performance baseline

Created `nexus-common/benches/crypto_bench.rs` with 4 criterion benchmarks:

| Benchmark | Target | Baseline |
|---|---|---|
| `aes256gcm_encrypt_1kb` | `Crypto::encrypt` | ~2.5 us |
| `aes256gcm_decrypt_1kb` | `Crypto::decrypt` | ~2.5 us |
| `sealed_envelope_seal_1kb` | `SealedEnvelope::seal` | ~73 us |
| `sealed_envelope_open_1kb` | `SealedEnvelope::open` | ~76 us |

Added `bench` CI job that runs after `test` and reports results in
bencher format. Regression gating can be added as a follow-up via
`github-action-benchmark`.

Updated `nexus-common/Cargo.toml` with `criterion` dev-dependency and
`[[bench]]` section.
